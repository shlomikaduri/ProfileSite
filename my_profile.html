About Me

Hi All, Welcome to my profile page! I am an Electrical Engineer with over 20 years of experience in the high-tech industry. Recently, I relocated for work to Texas, where I continue to grow in my career.

Early Beginnings in Technology

I grew up in Herzliya, Israel, and later lived in Tel Aviv and Ramat Gan. My journey in technology started at a very young age. I began working with computers at 7 years old, when I first used a PC that included a hard disk (back in 1989). My friends and neighbors were fascinated by the technology, and I quickly became the go-to person to teach and repair computers. I even created an early "BBC" style internet site, before the internet was widely available! It ran on my Bezeq phone line, with a regular modem connected to my PC, and the calls were based on the 135 service.

Education and Military Service

During high school, I studied electronics, later completing a practical engineering degree. I then served in the Israeli Defense Forces in a development unit, working with advanced technologies and gaining extensive experience in C, embedded systems, CVI, and more.

Achievements and Patents

Over the past 20 years, I’ve written two patents for the United States, which are credited to my name. In 2001, I won third place in a national competition for a project that tested VDSL technology. Additionally, my engineering thesis project involved developing a complete system on a chip (SOC) that included VHDL development.

Current Role and Focus

In recent years, I have been working with Python, and I’ve built and managed a team of 8 engineers. Currently, I am a Global Manager and lead a team of developers across India, Israel, and the United States. My primary focus is on automation solutions for hardware products, with a strong emphasis on software and hardware integration, primarily using Windows.
